Drift Mayhem

This prototype started with a simple idea: 'Wouldn't it look awesome to strafe enemies while drifting around them?' By locking onto a target, your vehicle can drift in circles around enemies while unleashing a barrage of attacks. Turn every hostile roaming the wasteland into swiss cheese!
